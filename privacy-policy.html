Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 16, 2025

Friday Night Funky ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website providing free online access to play Friday Night Funkin. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser Local Storage: The game may use your browser's local storage to save game progress, settings, and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Technical Information: When you access our website, our servers automatically log certain technical information including your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and access times. This is standard server log data used for site operation, security, and analytics.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

User-Provided Information

  • Comments and Ratings: If you choose to submit comments, ratings, or feedback, we collect and store the content you provide along with a timestamp. No account registration is required for these features.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and message content to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Friday Night Funkin online game service
  • To improve and personalize your gaming experience
  • To understand how users interact with our site and identify areas for improvement
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Use

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze data to help us operate and improve our service:

  • Content Delivery Networks (CDN): We use CDNs to serve game files, images, and other static content efficiently. CDNs may log access information such as IP addresses and browser types for performance and security purposes.
  • Analytics Services: We may use web analytics tools to track visitor behavior, page views, and traffic sources. These services help us understand how users interact with our site.
  • Advertising Partners: We may display advertisements through third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense). These partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing activity. You can opt out of personalized advertising through industry opt-out tools.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies and terms. We encourage you to review them.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website (e.g., hosting, CDN, analytics). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Browser-based game data stored in local storage remains on your device until you clear it. Server logs and analytics data are typically retained for a limited period for operational and security purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access and Control: You can access and manage your browser's local storage data at any time through your browser settings (Developer Tools → Application/Storage).
  • Clear Data: You can clear all locally stored game data by clearing your browser's site data (Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data).
  • Cookie Management: You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.
  • Opt-Out of Tracking: You can use browser extensions or privacy tools to block tracking scripts and third-party cookies.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers offer "Do Not Track" signals. While we respect user privacy, we may not respond to Do Not Track signals due to technical limitations.

For residents of certain jurisdictions (EU/EEA, California, etc.), additional rights may apply, including the right to request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our service is intended for general audiences and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent, as required by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

The game itself does not require account registration or personal information submission. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Parents and guardians: If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

International Data Transfers

Our servers and third-party service providers may be located in various countries around the world. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
